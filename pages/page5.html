TUKKI

Public relaxation building

The aim was to create little retreat building to get out of the cruel reality and relax and de-stress. Building provides mental and physical aid and allows mind and body to detach from outer world. Also a cocktailbar.
Tukki is constructed from four big masses, from which is removed round shaped area. The removing form a dome inside the four masses. The removed part expose wooden material inside the masses and form a relaxing space.
Outer material for the masses are solid concrete but insides are wooden all the way to the floor.
Entrance is simple and minimalistic. This solution makes Tukki easily approachable and open for all the visitors. Upon enterig the Tukki, opens up a huge dome with wooden surfaces and a lots of light from the river. A view to the river and skylights. The most important cocktailbar is located in the end of the building and it has its own terrace to enjoy the summer sun.
