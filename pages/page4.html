KAKU

Science museum Heureka student contest

Contest was to design a shelter to Heureka's outdoor exhibition area.
Kaku is constructed from clt-panels combined together. These panels make up stairs accessing upper level of the structure. Where one can observe whole outdoor exhibition
The shape is formed by environment around Kaku. The shed shapes alongside the flowing water and forms own organic shapes. Shelter opens up space for greenery rising from ground which further modify the shape.
Under the shelter, there are freely movable benches for every need. On top of the shelter, there are static benches so that they are not in the way of observer on top of the shelter.
With the shape, I aimed to attract whole family under and on top of the Kaku, wondering the structure and enjoying the shades and appreciating the view.
